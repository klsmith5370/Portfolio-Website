Who I am

Software Engineer based near NYC

Over the past six years, I have cultivated a career in e-commerce client services, honing my expertise in communication, leadership, and collaboration.

In 2020-2021, my journey took an exciting turn when I decided to explore the world of software development. My initial steps involved enrolling in a Python course at my local community college, followed by a preparatory course in JavaScript. This experience ignited a passion within me for the world of programming and software development.

Upon the recommendation of a friend, I eagerly applied for admission to the Grace Hopper Web Development bootcamp, a pivotal decision that would shape my future. During my time in the bootcamp, I received comprehensive training in both back-end development, using technologies like Express and PostgreSQL with Sequelize, and front-end development, working with React and React-Redux. This immersive experience provided me with a well-rounded understanding of full-stack development, equipping me with the skills needed to thrive in this dynamic field.

Upon successfully graduating from the bootcamp, my journey in software development has continued to flourish. I have further enriched my knowledge and proficiency in JavaScript, React, Redux, Express, and Sequelize with PostgreSQL. Armed with these newfound skills and a passion for engineering, I'm now poised to embark on a fulfilling career in the world of software development.
